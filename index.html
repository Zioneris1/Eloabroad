Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our visa-sponsored programs and process.

EloAbroad offers a premium, high-touch service with several key advantages:

  • Guaranteed placement with vetted employers
  • Stage-based pricing with no large upfront costs
  • End-to-end support including visa processing and relocation
  • Free certifications and training where required
  • Dedicated career advisor throughout your journey

Requirements vary by program:

  • English Teaching: No prior experience needed (we provide free TEFL certification)
  • Caregiving: No experience required (free training provided)
  • Hospitality: Prior experience preferred but not mandatory

We assess each candidate individually and provide any necessary training to ensure your success.

Our visa sponsorship process is comprehensive:

  1. After job placement, your employer files the necessary visa petition
  2. We prepare all your documentation and guide you through each step
  3. Our legal partners handle complex paperwork and requirements
  4. We provide interview preparation for embassy/consulate appointments
  5. Continued support until your visa is approved and you've relocated

Processing times vary by country but average 6-8 weeks for most programs.
