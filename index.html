FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about starting your international career with EloAbroad.

EloAbroad is 100% legitimate. We've removed expensive middlemen and built direct relationships with employers worldwide. Our $10 application fee covers basic vetting and unlocks our platform. The remaining $13 is only due after you're hired, paid by your new employer in most cases.

We currently place candidates in 30+ countries across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and North America. Popular destinations include Germany, Canada, UAE, Vietnam, and Sweden. Available countries depend on your qualifications and the job type.

Most positions require no prior experience - we provide free training for in-demand jobs like caregiving and TEFL teaching. For technical roles, relevant skills/education are needed. All candidates must be 18+, speak basic English, and pass a background check.

From application to relocation typically takes 2-6 months. Training takes 4-6 weeks (self-paced). Job placement averages 4-12 weeks after certification. Visa processing varies by country (2-8 weeks). Our fastest placement was 8 weeks total!

Our partner employers handle visa sponsorship for all placed candidates. We guide you through every step - document preparation, interviews, and relocation. Most positions include work permits, health insurance, and often housing assistance.
